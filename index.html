Triangle Tracker

The Triangle tracker is an application that will help us know whether a triangle is isosceles, equilateral, scalene or NOT a triangle at all. The
application takes the value that are inputed and thus tells whether the the values are for a given triangle or not.Equilateral triangle has all sides equal.Isosceles triangle has two sides equal.Scalene triangle has unequal sides.
